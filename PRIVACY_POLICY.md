# Propify Brokerage Agency — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

## 1. Who we are

Propify Brokerage Agency ("Propify", "we", "us") is a real estate brokerage agency operating in Egypt. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with us, including through our WhatsApp AI assistant, MAYA.

## 2. Information we collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp (including through Click-to-WhatsApp ads on Meta platforms), we may collect:

- Your name and phone number (as provided to WhatsApp)
- The content of your messages to us
- Information about the property or project you are asking about
- Details you choose to share to help us assist you, such as budget range, preferred area, or unit type

## 3. How we use your information

We use this information to:

- Respond to your inquiries about real estate projects and units
- Recommend properties that match your stated needs
- Send you official project files you request (brochures, price lists, master plans, etc.)
- Connect you with a human sales representative when needed
- Improve our services

## 4. How we store your information

Your conversation data is stored securely and used only for the purposes described above. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Sharing of information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

- With our own sales team, to follow up on your inquiry
- Where required by law

## 6. Your rights

You may ask us at any time to:

- Tell you what information we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your information, subject to legal record-keeping requirements

To exercise these rights, contact us directly through WhatsApp or at propifyegy@gmail.com.

## 7. Data retention

We retain conversation data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this link.

## 9. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at propifyegy@gmail.com.
